# Analytics

The Analytics page is where you get insights into your components.

![Analytics page](../../images/analytics-page.png)

There are two tabs:

- **Popular Charts** — provided by default, giving you a high-level overview of your design system.
- **Saved Dashboard** — the collection of charts you and your workspace members configured and saved.

## Sections

- [Popular charts](./popular-charts.md) — what each default chart shows
- [Create custom charts](./create-custom-charts.md) — build your own analyses
- [Save charts to dashboard](./save-charts-to-dashboard.md) — keep custom charts around
- [Share charts and dashboards](./share-charts-and-dashboards.md) — share with teammates or publicly
- [Download chart data](./download-chart-data.md) — export to CSV
